Hardware Identification Chart

PCI

PCI is a parallel bus, synchronous to a single bus clock. Attached devices can take either the form of an integrated circuit fitted onto the motherboard itself or an expansion card that fits into a slot.

Mother Board

The Motherboard itself is a printed circuit board that allows the CPU, RAM, and all other computer hardware components to communicate with each other.

Hard Drive

The Hard Drive is your computers storage. Everything you download on your computer is saved to that one point.

Network Card

A Network Card is an electronic device that connects a computer to a computer network.

DVD Drive

A DVD Drive is an optical disc drive that reads and writes all common CD and DVD formats.
